Thousands of accommodation units in Stockholm just for students

Stockholm has more student housing than any other Swedish city. And we have more student housing than any other landlord in Stockholm! Join the student union to be eligible for SSSB accommodation.

SSSB has almost 8000 rooms and apartments all over Stockholm. You can choose between a room in a students' corridor, a one-roomed apartment with a kitchenette or larger apartments both in the centre of the city and in nearby suburbs.

Put yourself on the waiting-list today to increase your chances of getting the student accommodation you want. And remember to join within 90 days!

Do you wish to book accommodation at SSSB?
In order to book accommodation at SSSB you must be on our waiting list. To be on the waiting list you need to be at least 16 and not more than 54 years old. You must also be a student in Stockholm and be a member of a student union affiliated to the Stockholm Federation of Student Unions (SSCO).
